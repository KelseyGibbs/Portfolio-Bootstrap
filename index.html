About Me

I am embarking on a transition from culinary arts to software development. Pastry and bread have been a long time passion but I am ready to look towards other passions to bring me the challenge I seek and the quality of life being a chef does not afford me. The more I learn about coding and the logic and attention to detail involved, the more I think pastry is a pretty good background for a software developer; I am equipped with an acute attention to detail, patience, ability to multi-task, and an innate mathematical ability that has always helped me with bread ratio's and problem solving logic. I am looking forward to applying what I learn over the next several months.
